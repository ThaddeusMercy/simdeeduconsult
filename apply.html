5 Steps to Applying Successfully
Contact Us
Send a message to us via Whatsapp or Email or call us directly. You will be asked to indicate your choice of study programme and preferred location(s) among other relevant information.
We Advice You
We provide accurate professional advice to meet your study requirements. Based on your feedback, we advise you on the most suitable study programme at our partner schools. We share a list of possible programmes and schools with you.
You Make Selections
Select your preferred country, school, and programme. You select your preferred study location, school, and programme. We guide you in filling out the application to the partner school and preparing all necessary academic documentation.
We Follow up
We follow up with the school and provide feedback to you. We provide continuous feedback to you to ensure that your application goes through successfully.
Fees Payment
Once an unconditional offer for admission is received, we provide you visa counseling and advice on how to pay your tuition and accommodation deposits.
Travel Assistance
We provide seamless travel arrangements for students including pre-departure briefing and arrangement with your school to pick you up at the airport.
